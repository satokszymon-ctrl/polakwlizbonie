Thinking about moving to sunny Portugal? Let me tell you what it's really like.

What the job is

Teleperformance runs projects for many large, well-known tech brands: customer service, technical support, content moderation. The tasks, language and pay depend on the project.

What you actually do

  • Help customers over email, chat or phone
  • Resolve tickets and explain things in plain language
  • Technical support on some projects
  • Or content moderation (reviewing material on platforms), a common start for young people with no experience

What you need

  • 18+ and the right to work in the EU
  • A conversational level of a language in demand (e.g. Polish, Ukrainian, Italian) plus English
  • Basic computer skills and good communication are a plus
  • Most roles require no prior experience or degree

Your language is your asset

One language is enough to start. The company is currently looking for people with Polish, Ukrainian and Italian, among others. And every extra language, like German, French or Dutch, means more projects to choose from and a higher rate. The more languages you speak, the better.

Work mode

Depends on the project. Teleperformance's main offices in Portugal are in Lisbon and Porto.

Remote Hybrid On-site (Lisbon, Porto)

From me

I'm on a very chill project myself, I only handle emails. Phone support is definitely harder, but it's normal customer service / helpdesk. There are also content moderation projects: you check whether content follows the platform's rules, usually a very calm job.

Relocation and housing

This is the core of why this job makes sense if you want to move.

Relocation support

Teleperformance helps with the move and can offer a room in a furnished company apartment (kitchen, living room, internet). Utilities are covered by the company. The cost of the room is deducted from your salary, but it's usually cheaper than renting on your own.

Or your own place

You can skip the company room. Then you get roughly 200 € more in hand every month, but you find housing yourself. The company room is usually the easier start if you don't know anyone in Portugal.

✈
Flight reimbursement: the company reimburses your flight to Portugal, usually after about 9 months of work.

What to expect

This is the path from leaving your CV to arriving in Portugal. The first step happens here, with me.

2. I submit you at the company
